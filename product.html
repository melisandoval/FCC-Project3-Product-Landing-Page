    • About Cool Dogs

    Cool Dogs is a company founded by two sisters who believe that our little canine friends deserve as much love as we can give them. That's why we create clothes to keep them warm and beautiful! 

    After creating many clothes for the dogs in our family, we decided to create Cool Dogs to share our designs with the world. All of our products are handmade with love and have soft and quality materials. We hope all the dogs and their human friends enjoy very much their Cool Dogs piece!
